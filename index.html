umcmx

Umcmx is an Artificial Inteligence oriented art studio run by London based artist Cemre Karakas. The studio creates multimedia artworks which span various media and art fields from digital paintings and graphics, digital collages, and procedurally generated 3D models.

Karakas's work revolves around human-machine interaction, machine interpretation and digital transformation of the analogue. Karakas treats automata as a self-transformative medium, and data as a landscape to be distilled and interpreted as art.
